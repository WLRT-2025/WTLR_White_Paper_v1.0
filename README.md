# WLRT — Wave Liquidity Redistribution Theory

## Overview

**Wave Liquidity Redistribution Theory (WLRT)** is a theoretical research framework
for modeling financial market dynamics through the redistribution of liquidity
in price space.

WLRT treats price not as a primary driver of market behavior, but as a **secondary
observable** emerging from the internal dynamics of liquidity distribution and flow.

The project aims to provide a **causal, geometric, and structural perspective**
on market behavior, independent of predictive or trading-oriented approaches.

## Project Structure

The WLRT project is organized into the following core components:

### White Paper

- **WLRT White Paper**  
  Conceptual and explanatory presentation of the theory.  
  Introduces motivation, core ideas, qualitative interpretation, and scope.

Repository:  
https://github.com/WLRT-2025/WLRT/WLRT_White_Paper

### Technical Appendix

- **WLRT Technical Appendix**  
  Formal mathematical and theoretical supplement to the White Paper.  
  Contains definitions, axioms, assumptions, and core equations.

Repository:  
https://github.com/WLRT-2025/WLRT/Technical_Appendix

### Future Components (planned)

- empirical studies and simulations,
- extensions to nonlinear dynamics,
- adaptive and multi-dimensional price-space models,
- comparative analysis with existing market theories.

These components will be released as separate, clearly scoped documents.

## Scope and Philosophy

WLRT is intended as:

- a theoretical research framework,
- a causal model of market structure,
- a foundation for analytical and empirical exploration.

WLRT is **not** intended for:

- price prediction,
- trading signal generation,
- investment or financial advice.

# Русский

## Обзор проекта

**Wave Liquidity Redistribution Theory (WLRT)** — это теоретическая исследовательская
модель, предназначенная для описания динамики финансовых рынков через
перераспределение ликвидности в ценовом пространстве.

В рамках WLRT цена рассматривается не как первичный драйвер рыночного поведения,
а как **вторичная наблюдаемая величина**, возникающая из внутренней динамики
распределения и потоков ликвидности.

Проект нацелен на формирование **причинно-следственного, геометрического и
структурного взгляда** на рыночную динамику, независимого от прогнозных
или торговых подходов.
